Save The Ocean


What is happening to the oceans?

  • People are not recycling plastic
  • Trash is being disposed in oceans instead of in landfills
  • people leave their trash on the streets which lead to storm drains that go to oceans
  • Animals are dieing because of:
    • Swallowing plastic
    • Getting stuck in plastic
    • living in bad polluted water


How Can We Fix This?

  • Do not throw trash on the streets
  • Make sure that your trash gets disposed properly
  • Encourage other people to watch what they throw away
  • Recycle what is plastic, paper, and glass
